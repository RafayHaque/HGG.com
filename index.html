Haque Group Global: A Legacy of Excellence Since 1947

Pioneers in Biscuits, Confectionery, Soap, and Battery Manufacturing

Founded in 1947, Haque Group Global has established itself as a formidable presence in the manufacturing world. Our journey began as the esteemed distributor for the British biscuit titan, Huntley & Palmers, and the renowned battery manufacturer, Union Carbide. A year later, in 1948, we expanded our operations to Dhaka, marking the beginning of a remarkable legacy.

Over the decades, Haque Group Global has been at the forefront of innovation and quality in producing biscuits, confectioneries, soaps, and batteries. Our products have not only satisfied customers but also set industry benchmarks.

In 2017, a new chapter unfolded when Mr. Rezaul Karim, Mr. Abul Khayer, and Mr Raju Ahmed took the helm of the company. Their combined expertise in leadership, industry experience, and profound knowledge has been pivotal in steering the company towards new horizons. In 2023, these visionary leaders acquired majority ownership from the founding Haque family, cementing their commitment to the company's growth.

As we move forward, Haque Group Global remains dedicated to its roots while embracing innovation. Our mission is to reclaim our former glory and go beyond, driven by our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and sustainable practices. Join us as we continue to build on our rich history and pave the way for a future filled with groundbreaking achievements.
